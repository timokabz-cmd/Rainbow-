About Our School

We don't just teach children —
we raise them.

Rainbow Elementary School in Mutundwe, Kampala is one of the area's most trusted nursery and primary schools. We have committed to one belief: every child who walks through our gates deserves the very best start in life — academically, morally, and socially.

Our classrooms are vibrant, our teachers are devoted, and our community is strong. We follow the Uganda National Curriculum from Baby Class through Primary Seven, ensuring every pupil is always on track.

Admissions

Enrol Your Child Today

Our process is simple, welcoming, and designed around you and your family.

1

Visit Our School

Come to Kabawo Zone, Kweba Road, Mutundwe. Our team welcomes walk-ins Monday to Friday from 8 AM. See the classrooms, meet the teachers, ask any questions.

2

Collect & Submit Your Form

Pick up an admission form from our office and return it with the required documents. We review every application personally.

3

Assessment (Primary Levels)

A brief, friendly assessment for Primary classes helps us place your child where they will thrive and grow with confidence.

4

Confirm & Welcome

Settle admission and term fees to secure your child's place. On their first morning, our team will be there to welcome them warmly.

Documents Required

✓
Birth Certificate or Declaration of Age
✓
Passport photographs — 4 copies
✓
Previous school report card (if applicable)
✓
National ID or Passport of parent / guardian
✓

✓
Completed admission form
